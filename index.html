About Me
My name is Jon Canning. I am a student of Mayo College of Further Education from Ireland currently enrolled in the EU Erasmus+ program. This website was designed and created as part of a task at my work placement in Gut Wehlitz campus. This website serves as a photo gallery for my time spent here in Leipzig.
